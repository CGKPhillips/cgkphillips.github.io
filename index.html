Firelight Tales - to kindle imagination!

Performed by storyteller Cindy Phillips

Cindy travels around Arkansas sharing her love of old-fashioned, oral storytelling with folks of all ages. She has performed in schools and libraries where listeners are eager to hear tales of long ago and far away.

Cindy is on the Arkansas Arts Council's Arts On Tour and Arts In Education artist rosters. She is a teaching artist with Arkansas Learning Through the Arts (ALTTA), using oral storytelling to promote literacy in classrooms and libraries. Here are a few testimonials:

Cindy's growing repertoire of over 50 stories includes folktales and fairy tales from all over the world, most of which are suitable for all ages. Themed programs include Halloween Tales, Native American/Indigenous Peoples Stories, African Tales, Fairy Tales of Mystery and Magic, and World Folktales.
